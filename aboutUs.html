Who we are?

We are a group of six friends from Ranchi, Jharkhand, students of 2nd year in BSc IT Course, currently studying at Dr. Shyama Prasad Mukherjee University. Our project aims to provide affordable, nutritious meals to everyone while working to eradicate hunger in our community. Inspired by the disparity between food waste and food scarcity, we developed a food mess that not only serves delicious meals but also runs free meal programs, food drives, and educational workshops. Despite challenges such as sourcing fresh ingredients and ensuring consistent delivery, our dedication to health and hygiene standards has helped us overcome these obstacles. The outcome is a sustainable, community-driven initiative that supports local farmers, involves volunteers, and ensures no one goes to bed hungry. Our main theme is "Nourishing the body and soul, one meal at a time," reflecting our commitment to both culinary excellence and social impact.
